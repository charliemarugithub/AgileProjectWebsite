Our Classes

  • Aerobics

    This is a form of physical exercise that combines rhythmic aerobic exercise with stretching and strength training routines with the goal of improving all elements of fitness.

  • Boxing

    The combat sport which involves punching a padded surface or another person. This involves wearing protective gloves and if your interested we can offer ways into amateur boxing.

  • Yoga

    A group of physical, mental and spiritual practices or disciplines which originated in ancient India. This is a type of exercise which moves the body into various positions in order to become more fit or flexible, to improve your breathing, and to relax your mind.

  • Pilates

    Similar to Yoga but wanting to move more then Pilates is for you and this will keep going at a steady pace while encompassing the similar actions of Yoga.

  • Tai Chi

    A Chinese martial art practiced for both its defence training, its health benefits and meditation, if your after something that ties martial arts and meditation then this is for you since it can all be done anywhere at any time.

  • Spinning

    This is indoor cycling and is a form of exercise which focuses on endurance, strength, intervals, high intensity and recovery. If your not one who likes biking in the elements then this is the one for you.
